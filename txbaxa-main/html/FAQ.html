Frequently Asked Questions

Your top questions answered below.

What is the purpose of the organization?

BAXA strives to unite like-minded students interested in the intersection between technology and business by facilitating the development of skills that can be utilized in a real-world business environment.

Who can join the organization?

Membership is open to undergraduate students at UT Austin regardless of major, year, or level of technical experience.

What are the benefits of joining?

Benefits include professional workshops, networking events, coding escape rooms, and access to our social network of alumni and peers.

How can I keep up with BAXA events?

We regularly post updates on Instagram @txbaxa. Members can also join our Slack and mailing list for weekly updates.
